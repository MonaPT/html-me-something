Few books I cherish most:

I have too many favorite books to list. One of my favorite quotes about books is "A mind needs books as a sword needs a whetstone" by Tyrion Lannister from Game of Thrones.

One of the biggest reasons why I happen to fall in love with a book is if/when I could relate to any of the major characters of the book which are also inspiring

Listed below are a few books which I cherish most for different reasons. I happened to read each of them at different phases of my life starting from my childhood. Each of them has had a certain impact on my character, personality, my thought process and my take on life.

Adventures of Tom Sawyer by Mark Twain
Heidi by Johanna Spyri
Sherlock Holmes - The Hound of The Baskervilles by Sir. Arthur Conan Doyle
Harry Potter Series by J.K. Rowling
Pride and Prejudice by Jane Austen
The Perks of Being A Wallflower by Stephen Chbosky
North and South by Elizabeth Gaskell

Adventures of Tom Sawyer by Mark Twain

Adventures of Tom Sawyer was the first ever novel I read in my childhood. It brought out the adventurous streak in me to light. So glad to be living in the state where the author of this novel, Mark Twain grew up.

Heidi by Johanna Spyri

I read Heidi in my early teens. Loved the animated version of this story so much that I had an urge to read the book. It's the story of a little girl called Heidi who lives in Alps. My love for nature began then. A trip to Swiss Alps in one of my bucket list.

Sherlock Holmes - The Hound of The Baskervilles by Sir. Arthur Conan Doyle

I read this book in my mid teens and immediately took a liking for reading more detective stories, mysteries and thrillers

Harry Potter Series by J.K. Rowling

I read The world famous Harry Potter series through my late teens. Thats when Fantasy and Fiction have become my most all time favorite genres.

The Perks of Being A Wallflower by Stephen Chbosky

This is a coming of age novel I read in my early twenties which I thoroughly enjoyed. I could relate to one of the characters in the book which is why it was close to my heart.

Pride and Prejudice by Jane Austen

I read this book in my late twenties. Fell in love with classics and period dramas ever since

North and South by Elizabeth Gaskell

North and South is a social novel set in 1800's. I admired the plot as well as both the lead characters of this book a great deal.
